About Me

Aerospace Engineer with propulsion/fluid systems focus

I recently graduated from Arizona State University with a Bachelor's in Aerospace Engineering and I graduate in December of 2025 with my Master's in Aerospace Engineering from Georgia Tech. I have spent most of my time in college focused on propulsion and fluid systems. Throughout college, I was heavily involved with Sun Devil Rocketry at ASU. My senior year, I was the president of the club as well as the propulsion team lead. At Georgia Tech, I'm a member of Yellow Jacket Space Program and am currently designing a high pressure custom poppet valve.

My industry experience includes internships at Blue Origin, Stoke Space, and Relativity. I interned with Blue Origin as a fluid test ops intern for New Shepard in the summer of 2023. In the summer of 2024, I interned with Stoke Space as a fluid systems design intern for stage 2 of their rocket Nova. This summer, I'm interning at Relativity as a fluid systems design intern working on stage 2 of Terran R. View the projects tab below to learn more about my projects.
