Blue Origin Summer 2023 Internship

At Blue Origin, I owned the acceptance test compaign for the solenoid valves that were part of the reaction control system for the New Shepard Crew Capsule. This test campaign included internal and external leakage tests with a 3600 psia MEOP, pull-in and drop out voltage tests, and vibration testing. The vibe testing was a 3 axis pressurized vibe test in which I measured leakage across the valve seat for vibration in each axis. I built all test setups from given P&IDs in the work order and owned the data collection by connecting pressure transducers, flow meters, and the power supply to a DAQ.

After completing the acceptance test campaign for the solenoid valves, I began an acceptance test campaign for custom piloted check valves. I proof tested these valves in a boom box to 4050 psia and conducted an internal and external leakage test. At the same time, I began qualification testing a flight ball latch system that was used to separate the creq capsule from the propulsion module. This testing included thermal cycling and blast testing.
