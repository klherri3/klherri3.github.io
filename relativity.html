Relativity Space Summer 2025 Internship

At Relativity Space, I own the Terran R stage 2 forward environmental control system. I maintain the temperature between the top of the stage 2 Lox dome and the payload attachment fairing. I designed and sized the pressurant supply lines connecting the raceway lines to the diffusers and the diffusers themselves to provide the needed mass flow rate for the system with a pressure drop budget. I also designed the brackets the diffusers mount to and selected the bolt pattern and bolts needed for the design. I am currently performing bolted joint analysis, structural analysis, and modal analysis in ANSYS.

After completing the acceptance test campaign for the solenoid valves, I began an acceptance test campaign for custom piloted check valves. I proof tested these valves in a boom box to 4050 psia and conducted an internal and external leakage test. At the same time, I began qualification testing a flight ball latch system that was used to separate the creq capsule from the propulsion module. This testing included thermal cycling and blast testing.
